Work With Me

I've designed a straightforward process to make working together easy — from first contact through final delivery. Here's how it works.

Step 1

Get in Touch

Fill out the intake form below or email me directly. Tell me about your project — what it is, where it's at in post, and what you're looking for from the grade. The more context the better.

Step 3

Send Me Your Footage

Once we're locked in, you'll send me your footage along with your edit timeline. Here's exactly what I need and how to send it.

What to send me:
  • Your locked edit timeline (XML, AAF, or DaVinci Resolve project)
  • All original camera files used in the edit (not transcodes)
  • Any LUTs you've been monitoring with
  • Reference images or stills for the look you're going for
SpecificationPreferredAlso Accepted
Codec Camera original (RAW, BRAW, ProRes, ARRIRAW) ProRes 4444, DNxHR 444
Format Original camera files .mov (ProRes), .mxf (MXF OP1a)
Resolution Native camera resolution Minimum 1080p, 4K+ preferred
Color Space Log / RAW (camera native) Rec.709 if no log available
Timeline XML from Premiere / FCPX AAF from Avid, DaVinci project file
How to send files: Use any of these — I'll provide a folder link once we're booked.
  • Google Drive / Dropbox — Share a folder with me (free, works for most projects)
  • WeTransfer Pro — Good for one-off deliveries up to 200GB
  • MASV — Best for very large files, fast global transfers
  • Physical drive — Ship a drive for feature-length projects (I'll return it)
Please don't send:
  • H.264 / H.265 compressed exports (quality loss, banding in grades)
  • Screen recordings or social media downloads
  • Footage with a baked-in LUT (send the raw + LUT separately)
Step 4

The Grade

I'll grade your project and send you a review link with the initial pass. You'll be able to leave timecoded notes directly on the video for any adjustments.

1
First Pass

Full grade based on your references and our discussion

2
Your Review

Watch the graded cut and leave timecoded notes

3
Revisions

I address your notes and send an updated review link

4
Final Delivery

Approved grade rendered in your delivery specs

How to give feedback:
  • I'll send you a Frame.io or Vimeo Review link — leave comments with timecodes directly on the video
  • Be as specific as possible: "0:42 — skin tones feel too warm" is much more useful than "make it cooler"
  • Group all your notes into one round rather than sending them piecemeal
Revision policy: Your package includes a set number of revision rounds. Each round = one consolidated set of notes from you, addressed in full. Additional rounds beyond your package are billed at $XX/round.
Step 5

Final Delivery

Once you approve the grade, I'll render and deliver your final files in whatever specs you need.

Standard delivery formats:
  • Broadcast / Festival: ProRes 422 HQ or DNxHR HQ in native resolution
  • Web / Streaming: H.264 or H.265, high bitrate
  • Social: Platform-optimized exports (16:9, 9:16, 1:1)
  • DCP: Available on request for theatrical delivery

Final balance is due on delivery. I'll send the remaining Square invoice along with your download link. Files delivered via Google Drive or Dropbox — same folder we've been using.
